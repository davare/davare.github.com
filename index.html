Abhijit Davare is a Principal Engineer in HW/SW Codesign within the CTO Team at Intel. Previously, he was with the Strategic CAD Lab at Intel. Abhijit received a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences from UC Berkeley in 2007. He was advised by Prof. Alberto Sangiovanni-Vincentelli and his dissertation topic was "Automated Mapping for Heterogeneous Multiprocessor Embedded Systems" . Abhijit received a M.S. in EECS from UC Berkeley in 2004 and a B.S. in Computer Engineering from the University of Pittsburgh in 2002.

Abhijit is a recipient of the Best Paper Award at the 2007 Design Automation Conference, and the Lim Predoctoral Prize in EECS at UC Berkeley in 2004. He has authored over 20 refereed journal and conference publications and has 8 issued patents. His interests are in the areas of HW/SW Codesign, electronic system-level design, and compilers.
