Abhijit Davare is a Research Scientist in the SoC Tools and IP Group at Intel Labs. Previously, he was a Research Scientist in the Platform Validation group at Intel Corporation. Abhijit received a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences from UC Berkeley in Fall 2007. He was advised at UC Berkeley by Prof. Alberto Sangiovanni-Vincentelli and his dissertation topic was "Automated Mapping for Heterogeneous Multiprocessor Embedded Systems" . Abhijit received a M.S. in EECS from UC Berkeley in 2004 and a B.S. in Computer Engineering from the University of Pittsburgh in 2002.
